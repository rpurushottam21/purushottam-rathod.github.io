About Me

I'm an Automotive Cybersecurity Engineer at L&T Technology Services, specialising in threat modelling, risk assessment, and compliance engineering for vehicle ECUs across automotive and off-highway domains.

My background in Electrical & Electronics Engineering gives me a hardware-first security perspective — a rarity in cybersecurity. I think about secure boot chains, hardware trust anchors, and cryptographic accelerators not as abstract controls but as physical silicon artefacts that can be attacked.

Beyond compliance work, I built a GenAI-based TARA automation framework that reduced delivery time by up to 80% per project cycle — because engineering velocity matters as much as engineering rigour.
